# gt.insightkit.in

> Gyan Therapy Affiliate Platform — a centralized affiliate storefront built by InsightKIT for Gyan Therapy.

## Overview

gt.insightkit.in is a dynamic affiliate platform designed for Gyan Therapy, one of India's largest technology YouTube channels with over 4.1 million subscribers.

The project solves a common problem faced by large content creators: affiliate links are often buried inside individual YouTube video descriptions, making them difficult for viewers to discover later. Instead of searching through hundreds of videos, visitors can browse a dedicated storefront that organizes every recommended product in one place.

The platform automatically updates whenever new products are added, requiring virtually zero maintenance after deployment.

## Features

- 🛍️ Centralized affiliate product storefront
- 🎥 Separate sections for dedicated review videos and multi-product videos
- 🔍 Easy discovery of products featured across the channel
- ⚡ Dynamic content powered by Supabase
- 🔄 Automatic website updates without code changes
- 📱 Responsive design for desktop and mobile
- 🛠️ Admin-friendly product management
- 🚀 Scalable architecture for thousands of products

## Platform Structure

### GT Store

A dedicated storefront for products that have their own review or recommendation videos, allowing viewers to quickly find items discussed in individual content.

### YouTube Products

A separate section for products featured inside roundup or multi-product videos where several recommendations appear in a single upload.

## How It Works

1. The team adds or edits products through the backend
2. Product information is stored in Supabase
3. The website automatically fetches the latest data
4. Visitors browse products by section
5. Clicking a product redirects users to the appropriate affiliate link

No manual website editing or redeployment is required when products change.

## Why It Exists

As a YouTube channel grows, older affiliate links become increasingly difficult for viewers to find. Valuable recommendations remain hidden inside video descriptions, reducing both user convenience and affiliate revenue.

This platform transforms scattered links into a searchable, organized storefront that improves product discoverability while giving the content team a simple system for managing recommendations.
